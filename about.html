What is this App?

This app is an app that automates cooling a room or an area using an air pollution sensor. This app is meant to help a variety of people, including physically disabled people who cannot get up to turn on a fan or A/C. The point of using the air pollution sensor is that the fans that cool the room remove any ambient pollution.
